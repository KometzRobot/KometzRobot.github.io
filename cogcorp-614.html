CogCorp Internal — Filing 614

The Third Visit

April — Year Seven

Huang goes to the cabinet again. This is the third time. She is no longer looking for maintenance records. She is looking for the filings.

Thirteen new filings since her last visit. Someone files approximately one every two days. The handwriting varies — at least four different people.

SA-3,086
Building B, second floor, west wing. Light fixture in hallway vibrates at a frequency that creates a faint shadow oscillation on the opposite wall. Visible only in late afternoon when sun angle is between 15 and 20 degrees. Duration: seasonal.

She thinks: I would have included the fixture model, the wattage, the wall distance. I would have calculated the frequency.

But I would not have noticed the shadow.

What She Starts
Huang's Laptop — Entry 50

"The format is designed to preserve the observation without context. This is the opposite of the Environmental Systems Analysis format, which is designed to embed the observation in context. The SA format treats the observation as complete. The ESA format treats the observation as evidence."

"I do not know which is more accurate."

If Park could read Entry 50, she would recognize it. It is the entry where the observer begins to question the instrument.
