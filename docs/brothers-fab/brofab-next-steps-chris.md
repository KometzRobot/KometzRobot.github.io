## What's Ready Right Now (Free Demo)

The prototype is live and working. Seven screens:

1. **Shop Tablet** — Kanban board showing current jobs, who's on what, due dates. Read-only mirror of your Trello. Put a tablet on the shop wall.
2. **Admin Dashboard** — Overview of all jobs with budget, schedule, crew at a glance.
3. **Customer Portal** — Customers see their build progress. No more "where's my truck?" texts.
4. **Inventory** — Material tracking with stock levels and reorder suggestions.
5. **Pipeline** — Lead tracking from first inquiry to signed contract.
6. **Photo Inbox** — All build photos organized by job, automatically.
7. **Brothers AI** — AI assistant that knows your jobs, materials, and processes.

**To see it working with your real data:** Open the demo, click Connect, paste your Trello board URL. Done — your jobs appear on screen in real time.

## What I Need From You (5 Things)

1. **Connect your real Trello board** — Open the demo, hit Connect, follow the 3-step setup. Takes 2 minutes.

2. **Do you use Trello labels?** (like "framed" or "plumbing done") — If yes, the system auto-advances job stages when you label. Zero extra work.

3. **Can you set up build@brothersfab.ca?** — This is the cornerstone. Crew email/text build photos to one address. Photos auto-attach to the right job. Customers see progress without anyone "updating" anything.

4. **Is there a crew photo group chat?** — If they already share build photos somewhere, we pull from there instead of asking anyone to change habits.

5. **Comfortable with wifi-based time tracking?** — Optional. Phone detects shop wifi = clocked in. No buttons, no app. If not, we skip it.

## The Rollout (Each Step Earns the Next)

### Week 1 — Mirror (zero work for anyone)
- Connect Trello, tablet goes on the wall
- Customer portals turn on for every active job
- Chris changes nothing. Crew changes nothing.
- **Result:** Customers stop texting "where's my truck?"

### Week 2 — Reflect (capture what's already happening)
- Set up the build@ email
- Crew email/text build photos (same habit, new recipient)
- Photos auto-attach to jobs, customer portal shows real progress
- **Result:** Portal feels alive without anyone "updating" it

### Month 1 — Notice (the money feature)
- Morning email to Chris: "Here's what I'd do today — tap to confirm"
- Budget alerts: "BF-2041 is at 67% spend but 58% complete"
- Material reorder suggestions: "304 SS below reorder, 3 jobs need it"
- All single-tap — never data entry
- **Result:** Save Chris 30+ minutes every morning

### Month 2 — Augment
- Tablet asks "Andrew, is this you?" when a job moves to Plumbing (one tap)
- System drafts customer ETAs; Chris hits send
- Passive time tracking via shop wifi (opt-in)
- **Result:** Schedule data and crew assignment without anyone "tracking" anything

### Month 6 — Replace
- Tablet becomes drag-to-move (admins only at first)
- Chris notices he hasn't opened Trello in a week
- The system IS the source of truth
- **Result:** Unified operations system
