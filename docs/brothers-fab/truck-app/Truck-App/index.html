Brothers Fabrication — Truck Configurator

This is an early draft that's evolved from a demo build. Things may be rough around the edges — that's expected.

Quick Start

  • Click an equipment item in the sidebar to start placing it
  • Click inside the truck to place — R to rotate, V to cycle variant
  • Right-click an item to remove it
  • Scroll to zoom, right-drag to orbit, middle-drag to pan

Two Modes

  • Internal — Full toolset: cost breakdowns, compliance checks, BOM, power tracking, utility routing, build checklists. For the shop.
  • Customer — Simplified view: layout, equipment list, and quote request. What the customer sees.

Toggle modes with the button in the top bar.
