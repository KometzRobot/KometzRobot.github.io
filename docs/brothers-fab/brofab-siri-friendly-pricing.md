Hello. I put together some pricing for what we talked about. I wanted to keep it simple and easy to follow.


My Rates

I can work at three different rate levels depending on the complexity of work. Marketing and content work runs thirty-five to forty-five dollars per hour. Design and web development runs fifty to sixty dollars per hour. Custom systems and AI work runs sixty to seventy-five dollars per hour.

For reference, Calgary agencies typically charge one hundred to two hundred dollars per hour. AI consultants usually charge two hundred to two hundred fifty per hour. My rates are competitive but fair for the quality of work you get.
