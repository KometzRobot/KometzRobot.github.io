## Contract Service Concepts

### Tier 1: Quick Wins ($500–$2,000 each, one-time)

**1. Google Business Optimization**
- Claim/optimize Google Business Profile
- Systematic review collection strategy
- Photo optimization for search visibility
- Local SEO keywords
- *Why it matters:* 43 reviews is great, but competitors may have more. Each review = more visibility for $79K+ food truck searches.

**2. Social Media Content Templates**
- Branded post templates for Instagram/Facebook (build progress, before/after, client spotlight)
- Content calendar with posting schedule
- Hashtag strategy for Calgary food truck + fabrication market
- *Why it matters:* Their work is visual — a food truck build is content gold. Systematic posting converts followers to leads.

**3. Professional Photography Package Coordination**
- Photo shoot of shop, team, and finished builds
- Drone footage of completed food trucks
- Portfolio page redesign with high-quality imagery
- *Why it matters:* At $79K–$139K per build, professional photos pay for themselves with one additional lead.

### Tier 2: Medium Projects ($2,000–$10,000, project-based)

**4. Automated Quote Request System**
- Web form → structured email/notification to Chris & Chris
- Customer uploads photos/specs, selects service type
- Auto-response with FAQ, timeline expectations, portfolio link
- Optional: integration with Google Sheets or simple CRM
- *Complexity:* Medium. Nick could maintain it after setup.
- *Why it matters:* Reduces back-and-forth on initial inquiries. Captures leads 24/7.

**5. Food Truck Build Configurator**
- Interactive web tool: customer selects truck type, layout, appliances, finishes
- Generates rough estimate and visual layout
- Captures lead info for follow-up
- *Complexity:* Medium-high.
- *Why it matters:* Differentiator. No other Calgary fabricator offers this. Qualifies leads before first conversation.

**6. AI-Powered Website Chatbot**
- 24/7 chatbot trained on Brothers Fabrication services, pricing, FAQ
- Answers common questions (turnaround time, what's included, financing)
- Captures leads and routes to email
- Built on existing AI frameworks (low ongoing cost)
- *Complexity:* Medium.
- *Why it matters:* Food truck buyers research at night. Chatbot captures those leads instead of losing them.

**7. Customer Project Portal**
- Web dashboard where current build clients can see progress photos
- Milestone updates (frame complete, plumbing, electrical, wrap, delivery)
- Reduces "how's my truck coming?" calls
- *Complexity:* Medium.
- *Why it matters:* Premium customer experience for premium prices. Builds trust and referrals.

### Tier 3: Ongoing Contracts ($500–$2,000/month)

**8. Social Media Management**
- 3-4 posts/week (build progress, team spotlight, industry news, client features)
- Engagement management (reply to comments, DMs)
- Monthly analytics reports
- Paid ad campaigns for seasonal pushes
- *Why it matters:* Consistent posting = consistent leads. Their current posting appears sporadic.

**9. SEO & Content Marketing**
- Monthly blog posts (food truck industry trends, Calgary food scene, build guides)
- YouTube/TikTok build process videos (time-lapse)
- Backlink building from Calgary business directories and food truck forums
- *Why it matters:* "Food truck builder Calgary" is a high-intent search. Ranking #1 means first call.

**10. AI-Assisted Operations Support**
- Internal tools: project scheduling, inventory tracking, client communication templates
- Automated follow-up emails after quote requests
- Automated review request emails after project completion
- *Complexity:* Builds over time.
- *Why it matters:* With 9 employees and high-ticket projects, even small efficiency gains compound.
