How This Would Actually Work

All templates are customizable — real photos from the shop would replace the placeholders. Content and posting would be part of the ongoing monthly management, overseen by Chris P. The system would also be set up to support other content and media creators as Brothers grows — so if you bring on a videographer or photographer down the road, they'd plug right into the same brand framework.
