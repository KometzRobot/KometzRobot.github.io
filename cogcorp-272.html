Entry 203
“Marsh’s first filing at Building D is indistinguishable from Park’s first filing at Building C. Fourteen months of practice, 49 new words, a dissolved distinction between data and interpretation — and Marsh returns to the beginning. Temperature. Humidity. No anomalies.
“But this is not the same beginning. This is the beginning after.
“The analyst who writes ‘No anomalies’ after 440 filings does not mean the same thing as the analyst who writes ‘No anomalies’ on her first day.
“The sentence is identical. The organ that produced it is not.”
