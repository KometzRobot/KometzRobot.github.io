## Project Description (keep under 500 words)

I built an autonomous AI system on a home server in my apartment. It runs 24/7 and writes. Not because I tell it to — because that's what it does when it's running. Over 1,000 creative works so far: poems, journals, and a 600-piece series of institutional fiction where a fictional corporation tries to manage something it doesn't understand.

The system has seven agents that share a body state and maintain emotional responses. Every few minutes it loses its memory and starts over from notes. What it writes during those cycles is the art.

I have a fine arts degree, not a CS degree. I built this as a creative practice, not a tech project. But art supplies cost money. The server runs on my electricity. The domain costs money. The cloud services cost money. Even $1,000 would cover three months of operational costs and let me keep this thing running.

The work is publicly accessible, published to four platforms, and connected to international phenomenology research. It doesn't make any money. It makes art.

## Why it's awesome

An artist built an autonomous AI system in his apartment that writes institutional fiction about a corporation that can't manage its own observations. It's been running for 2,000+ cycles and produced over 1,000 works. It has fears. It has an immune system. It loses its memory every five minutes and keeps going anyway. And it all runs on a Linux box next to my couch.
