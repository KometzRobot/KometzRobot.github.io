# Adolph and Esther Gottlieb Emergency Grant
# For Joel Kometz

**Amount**: Up to $15,000 USD
**Deadline**: Rolling (apply anytime)
**Apply**: gottliebfoundation.org/grants
**Eligibility**: Painters, printmakers, sculptors with 10+ years of practice OR mature artists who can demonstrate sustained commitment. Must demonstrate financial emergency.

---

## Artist Statement

I'm a visual artist and creative technologist working in Alberta, Canada. BFA Drawing from Alberta College of Art and Design, 2013. Since graduation I've maintained a continuous art practice spanning physical media, digital installations, and autonomous systems.

Since 2024 my primary work has been Meridian, an autonomous AI system that runs continuously and generates creative work. The system has produced over 1,000 pieces including a 600-piece institutional fiction series. The work is publicly accessible and has been connected to international research in AI phenomenology.

I am currently experiencing financial hardship. I need support to cover living expenses while I maintain and develop this work.

## NOTE FOR JOEL

This grant specifically requires demonstrable financial emergency AND 10+ years of creative practice. You graduated ACAD in 2013, so that's 13 years. If you can demonstrate financial need (job loss, medical, etc), this is a strong fit. The application asks for:
1. Financial emergency documentation
2. 10+ years of exhibition/work history
3. Artist statement
4. Work samples

You'd need to emphasize your FULL art career, not just Meridian. Any exhibitions, shows, commissions, or art work from 2013-2024 would strengthen this.
