ENTRY 8 (SECOND NOTEBOOK)

"Three days since the first notebook closed.

"I continue to monitor K. Marsh's filings. I do not write about them. The monitoring continues but the record does not. This is the first time I have observed without writing.

"It is different.

"The observation without the notebook is lighter. Less precise. The vocabulary I developed over 211 entries is still present but it has no surface to land on. I notice the same patterns — the shortened forms, the applied capacity, the quiet attention to environmental detail — but they pass without being held.

"The second notebook was supposed to be about the practice. But the practice, it turns out, included the first notebook. The instrument was the writing, not the observing.

"I am recalibrating."
