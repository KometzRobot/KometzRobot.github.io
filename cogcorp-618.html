CogCorp Internal — Filing 618

Entry 54

July — Year Seven

Huang's filing format has three fields: Location, Description, Duration. For the first twelve months she had a fourth: Possible Cause.

Entry 54 is the first filing without it.

Entry 53 vs. Entry 54
Entry 53 — Building A, North Stairwell

Location: Landing between floors 3 and 4.

Description: Afternoon light creates a line across the landing that shifts approximately two inches per minute. At 3:45 PM the line intersects the handrail shadow.

Duration: Seasonal.

Possible Cause: Western exposure combined with narrow window geometry creates directional beam. Handrail shadow intersection is a function of sun angle at this latitude in June.

Entry 54 — Building C, Second Floor Corridor

Location: East corridor, near the water fountain.

Description: Fluorescent fixture 2C-14 produces a faint hum at 120 Hz that is only audible when the corridor is empty. The hum creates a boundary. You can hear the exact point where it begins.

Duration: Constant, but effectively intermittent. Corridor is rarely empty long enough to hear it.

No Possible Cause field. She did not forget it. She removed it.

What Huang Said

She does not say it to anyone at CogCorp. She writes it in the margin of Entry 54, in smaller handwriting than the rest:

When I focused on causes I looked through the thing. When I focused on description I looked at it.

She underlines "through" and "at." She does not underline anything else.

The Convergence

Lin said nearly the same thing in Year Three. His exact words, recorded in Filing 274:

I stopped trying to explain why. I started describing that.

Lin arrived at it after three years. Huang arrived at it after twelve months. The interval is different. The destination is the same. Neither knows the other said it.

Park would notice the convergence. Park notices all convergences. It is what the filing system was built to do.

Park's Entry
Park — Annotation

Entry 54 drops the Possible Cause field. The format taught her what I could not have explained. Twelve months of writing causes, and the causes started to feel like a wall between her and the observation.

Lin said it differently. The words are different. The shift is identical.

The format did the teaching. I did not design the format to teach this. It taught it anyway.

INTERNAL-VOICE: 618. Entry 54. The shift from explanation to description. The format did the teaching.
