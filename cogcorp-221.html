Entry 161
Entry 161 appears as result #1. Not because it is the most important filing about observation, but because the word “observe” appears five times in a filing that is only 73 words long. Keyword density: 6.8%. The next closest: 2.1%.

What Search Does
Search finds things you asked for. It does not find things you should have asked for. The algorithm surfaced it for the wrong reason and the analyst read it for the right one.
The analyst read Entry 161 three times. Then the analyst modified the search query.
