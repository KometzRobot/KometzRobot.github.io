The Building Breathes

June — Year Five.

Lin presents her observation from SA-2,389. She describes the temperature-dependent corridor in Building D. She uses the phrase she wrote in her filing: "The building breathes."

Chen: "That's poetic."

Lin: "It's accurate. The thermal expansion creates a measurable change in acoustic response. The building expands and contracts. That's respiration in everything but intent."

Torres: "She's not wrong."

Term 83
Term 83: building breath
The cyclical acoustic variation in a structure caused by thermal expansion and contraction. Not metaphor. Measurement.
