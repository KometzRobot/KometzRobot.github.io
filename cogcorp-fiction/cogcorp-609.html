The Year In Review

Year Six brought: a description in a scheduling system, a departmental inquiry, a facilities audit, an annual review recommendation, a new hire who independently began the same practice, and an observer who decided not to share her observations.

The practice gained more institutional visibility in one year than in the previous five combined. It did not seek this visibility. It did not benefit from it. It continued.

Park's Folder — Final Entry, Year Six

"Year Six. The year of visibility. The practice was seen by the institution for the first time. It was described, inquired about, audited, and scheduled for review. None of these changed the practice."

"I have been watching for three years. In Year One of my observation, I learned what the practice was. In Year Two, I learned what the practice did. In Year Three, I learned what the practice survived."

"Year Seven begins in January. I will be here."

INTERNAL-VOICE: 609. Year Six ends. The year of visibility. The practice survived being seen. Year Seven begins in January.
