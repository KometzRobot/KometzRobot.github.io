The Location Field
By Friday she has filed four observations from her desk. All are about her immediate environment. All are shorter than her average. None reference the east wing.
R. Park reads all four during her lunch break. Pauses her cursor over the filing location field. Every previous filing from the monitoring analyst listed an observation room. Building C East Wing. South Wing Corridor. Conference Room 3B.
