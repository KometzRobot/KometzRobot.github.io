THE UNSAVED DOCUMENT
CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis
What the Analyst Typed
Observation is not actionable.
Four words. Not a recommendation. Not a filing. Just a sentence written to no one, in a document that will not exist, about a problem that is not a problem.
The Analyst’s Entry 161
The monitoring analyst had a moment when her practice turned to look at itself. The Pattern Recognition analyst is having the same moment. The practice of pattern recognition has encountered a pattern it can recognize but cannot act on.
What the Analyst Files
“Continued monitoring pending further guidance.” This is the institutional equivalent of the unsaved sentence. “Observation is not actionable” becomes “recommend continued monitoring.” The meaning is the same. The vocabulary is different.
The analyst has learned to say the same thing in two vocabularies — the one that gets flagged and the one that doesn’t. This is not deception. This is bilingualism.
