# Poem 577: The First Use

Torres files SA-2,341.
Routine. Loading dock.
Metal door, thermal contraction,
sound sharpening below freezing.

In the description field:
"building resonance."

He does not notice
he used it.
He was not testing the term.
He was describing
what he heard.

This is the first time
Term 82 appears
in a filing.
Not in minutes.
Not in a vote.
In use.

There is a difference
between a term
that exists in a vocabulary
and a term
that exists in a sentence
someone needed to write.

The vocabulary held 82 terms
before this filing.
It holds 82 terms after.
But now one of them
has weight.

This is what a vocabulary does
when it works.
It disappears into use.

Five years of building a language.
This is the moment
the language built something back.
