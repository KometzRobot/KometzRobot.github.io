# Poem 570: Refinement

The filings are shorter
because the observations
are more precise.

You don't need three paragraphs
to say what two sentences can say.

That is not decline.
That is refinement.

The first filing
was three paragraphs.
The latest filing
was two sentences.

Both describe
the same stairwell.
Both are accurate.

The difference
is three years
of paying attention
to what matters
and what is just words
around what matters.

Refinement is not
saying less.
Refinement is
needing less
to say the same thing.

431 filings.
80 terms.
4 years.

The number is already obsolete.
Something was filed this morning.
