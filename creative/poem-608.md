# Poem 608: The Asymmetry

Three unsigned filings
in six weeks.

The distance between noticing
and writing
is shrinking.

Not because she is hurrying.
Because the gap
between observation and record
is closing
on its own.

Her 0.7 is as correct
as my 0.8.
The difference is not error.
The difference is position.

The archive holds her observations
alongside ours
without distinction.

The format is democratic.
The culture is not.

We know her observations.
She does not know ours.

The asymmetry
is the last distance.
