# Poem 527: The Second Notebook

Twelve filings in nine years.
Forty-three entries in five months.
The difference is the system.

The system asks: is this anomalous?
The notebook asks: is this here?

One requires vocabulary.
The other requires a windowsill.

"If I put them in the system they would stop."

She knows this the way you know
that opening the oven
changes the temperature
of what you're trying to measure.

The notebook is not part of the monitoring system.
The notebook is the monitoring system
that the monitoring system
could not build.
