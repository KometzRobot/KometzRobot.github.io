# Poem 510: The Answer

Kim brings a folder.
Faded blue.
Elastic band.

"I've been thinking
about question forty-four."
"For three months?"
"For three months."

A maintenance request from 2014.
Nine years before the monitoring position.
Nine years before the vocabulary.

Reeves, D. Accounting.

The heating vent
in Conference Room 2B
clicks at 11:40 AM.
People hear the click
and know the morning session
is ending.

When the vent was serviced
and the clicking stopped,
two people asked
whether the schedule had changed.

"I think the building
is doing something
that we don't have
a word for."

Nine years before any of this.
Not a practitioner.
Not a monitoring analyst.
Just someone who noticed.

The practice existed
before we created visibility.
Reeves used a maintenance request
because there was no other format.
The form was wrong
but the observation was right.

Okafor writes the answer
below the question:
"The practice preceded the format.
The format gave the practice
a place to live."

She does not cross out
the question.

Answered questions
are still questions.
