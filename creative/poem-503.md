# Poem 503: The Translation

Filing compliance: 98.7%.
Filing volume: 728.
Staffing: one rotation, smooth.
Recommendation: continue.

Every sentence true.
None of them describe
what happened.

The 340% increase
is not from expanded protocols.
It is from twelve people
who learned to pay attention
to buildings.

The "strong familiarity"
was not familiarity
with standards.
It was familiarity
with the practice itself.

Martinez reads the report back.
The most accurate
and most misleading document
he has ever written.

Kim: "The translation
protects the original."

Approved in four minutes.
No one asks
a follow-up question.

Nakamura writes a note
she does not file:
"Described everything accurately
and nothing truthfully.
This is how a practice survives
inside an institution:
by being translatable
into the institution's language
without being reducible to it."

Some observations
are for the observer.
