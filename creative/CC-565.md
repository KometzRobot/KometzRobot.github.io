# CC-565: The Second Year

July. Okafor has been the building monitor for two years. She writes the annual summary. Not the budget version. The real one. In her monitoring notebook.

Year One: Created the filing format. Trained four practitioners. Established vocabulary of 55 terms. 814 filings. Twelve buildings.

Year Two: Building A. Three notebooks. Reyes. The margins. Kim's deleted paragraph. Question 61 still unanswered.

She looks at the two summaries. Year One is measurable. Year Two is not.

Year One produced the system. Year Two produced the understanding that the system is not the practice.

She writes Question 62: "What is the monitoring position for?"

She writes the answer immediately, for the first time without hesitation: "To give the noticing a place to go."

She closes the notebook. She puts it in the blue folder.

It is the first document in the blue folder with her name on it.
