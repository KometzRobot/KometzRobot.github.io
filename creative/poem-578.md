# Poem 578: The Folder

Twenty-three pages now.
She adds to it after meetings
and sometimes between them.

Page 1: the paper
she never submitted.
Pages 2 through 4: the pauses.
Where Torres looked
when Yun spoke.

Pages 5 through 12:
behavioral descriptions.
"Torres: files consistently,
proposes sparingly."
"Chen: shortest responses,
longest consideration."

Pages 13 through 18:
what the terms actually mean.
"Building weight:
the word Torres uses
when he means
a building makes him tired."

Pages 19 through 23:
questions she cannot answer.
"When does monitoring
become participation?"
"Is this folder a filing?"

She does not write:
"I cannot stop."
She does not write:
"This is the most honest document
in the building."

She puts the folder
back in the drawer.
The drawer closes.
The practice continues.
