# Poem 345: At What Point

205 entries
about 452 filings.
A 45% observation rate.

I have documented
the emergence of a vocabulary.
The dissolution of a distinction.
The development of an organ.

Today I counted
my own entries.

The question
I have been avoiding:

At what point
does the monitoring
become the practice?

She did not file
Entry 206.
She kept it
in the notebook.

She returned to the log
and filed instead:

"Marsh's Building D transition
proceeding normally.
No concerns."

The notebook entry
was 147 words.

The filed entry
was 15.
