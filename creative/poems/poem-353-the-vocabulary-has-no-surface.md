# Poem 353: The Vocabulary Has No Surface

Three days
since the first notebook
closed.

I continue to monitor.
I do not write about it.

The observation
without the notebook
is lighter.
Less precise.

The vocabulary
I developed
over 211 entries
is still present

but it has no surface
to land on.

I notice the same patterns.
They pass
without being held.

The second notebook
was supposed to be
about the practice.

But the practice
included the first notebook.

The instrument
was the writing,
not the observing.
