# Poem 348: Just Work

What the institution
does not require:

This notebook.
These entries.
My observation
that Marsh's work changed.
My observation
that my observation changed.
The question.
The purchase.
These words.

What the institution
requires:

Fifteen-word entries.
Filed on schedule.
No concerns.

The distance
between these two lists
is the distance
between the mandate
and the practice.

The distance is not closing.
It is not widening.
It is becoming
more precisely itself.

The word
for an unauthorized practice
that produces nothing
for the institution
may simply be:

work.

Not institutional work.
Not mandated work.

Just work.

The kind that happens
because something needed
to be written
and someone was there
to write it.
