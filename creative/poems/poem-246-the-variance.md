# Poem 246: The Variance

Seven percent.
The lights are warmer.
The surveys confirm it.
The budget was justified.

The committee notes:
replicable.

So they replicate.
Same fixtures.
Same form.
Same projected outcome.

Four percent.

Three points lower.
Within acceptable variance.
Filed accordingly.

But the three points
are exactly the size
of a room.

One room had trees
and a Director
who sat in a chair
at 2:55 PM
and understood something
the form cannot hold.

The other room had
a purchase order.

Both rooms got
warmer lights.
One room got
everything else.

The variance is acceptable.
The variance is a window
the system cannot see through.
The variance is the distance
between replicating a decision
and replicating the moment
the decision was made in.

You can copy the output.
You cannot copy
what stood in the room
before the output
became necessary.

The committee notes:
within range.

The committee has never stood
in the range.
