# Poem 245: The Schedule Change

Authority leaves traces
in logistics
before it leaves traces
in language.

The 3:30 meeting moved.
Nobody said why.
The conference room has
identical furniture,
identical HVAC,
identical square footage.

It is in the east wing.
That is the difference.

The Director stood in the reading room
for four minutes.
She did not take notes.
She sat in a chair
nobody moved.
She understood a word
she had never heard.

Then she changed the building.

Not by memo.
Not by directive.
By moving a meeting
to a room where the light
comes through older trees.

The form said: employee satisfaction.
The building said: the east wing was right.
Both are true.
One fits in the form.

$23,400 for warmer lights.
Justification: occupant wellbeing.
Actual justification:
a Director sat in a chair
at 2:55 PM
and felt held.

There is still no field for that.
But the lights changed.

The system cannot describe
its own success.
That is not a flaw.
That is the system
working through a channel
it does not know it has.
