# Poem 346: The Second Notebook

She purchased
a second notebook.

The first contains
206 entries
about Marsh's work.

The second
is for everything else.

Entry 1:
"I have started
a second notebook
because Entry 206
did not belong
in the first.

The first notebook
is about Marsh's work.
Entry 206
was about mine.

The distinction matters.

As long as my observations
stayed in one notebook,
I could maintain
that I was monitoring.

Entry 206 revealed
that I was also practicing.

The practice needed
its own space."

The first notebook
became simpler
after the second
began.
