# Poem 347: The Unauthorized Practice

147 words to 15.

I told myself
the reduction was
professional restraint.
Standard procedure.

But that is not
quite right.

The 15-word version
was not a reduction.
It was a different document.

The 147-word version
asked a question.

The 15-word version
closed the file.

Two practices.

One that monitors Marsh.
One that monitors
the monitoring.

They cannot share a notebook
because they do not share
a subject.

The first practice
has a mandate.

The second
does not.

No authorization.
No reporting chain.
No quarterly review.

It exists because
I continued writing
after the mandate
was satisfied.

I do not know
what to call
an unauthorized practice
that produces nothing
for the institution.
