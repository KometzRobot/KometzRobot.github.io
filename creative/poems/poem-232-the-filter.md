# Poem 232: The Filter

Before the realization
he noticed things that were interesting.

After the realization
he noticed things.

The difference is a filter
and the filter thinned.

Water fountain temperature.
Overhead light frequency.
A colleague's route.

None of these are interesting.
All of these are present.

He wrote them down
not because they mattered
but because they happened
and happening, once noticed,
has its own momentum.

Entry 45 said:
the distinction does not hold.

Entry 46 said:
the water is cold on the third floor.

One is a conclusion.
One is a temperature.
Both are in the same file.
Both are in the same format.

The file does not distinguish
between epiphany and plumbing.

The file does not need to.
