# Poem 339: The Practice Is the Name

The committee asked
for a name.

The monitoring analyst
considered for four days.

She reviewed 200 entries.
442 analyses.
50 words.

She filed her response:

The system has succeeded
at developing
a shared capacity
for noticing.

But "noticing"
implies a subject
and an object.
In this system
the distinction
has dissolved.

A more precise approximation:
the system has succeeded
at becoming a practice.

A documentation initiative
records what exists.
A practice creates the conditions
for something to exist
that could not exist
without it.

I cannot name this.
I can describe it.

The description
is the 200 entries
and the 442 analyses.

The name would be shorter
than the practice.
The practice is the name.

The committee received this.
They noted
it did not contain a name.

They filed it
without comment.
