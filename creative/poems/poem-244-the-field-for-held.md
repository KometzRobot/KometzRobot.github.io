# Poem 244: The Field for Held

There is no field for "held."
The form offers: location, measurement, reference.
The form does not offer:
the way filtered light
makes a room feel like the inside of something
that knows you are there.

They asked why the east wing feels different.
They meant: why do people linger.
They measured: UV transmission coefficients.
They concluded: 23% less direct sunlight.

Nobody reads past page four.

The monitoring analyst knew the answer
four months before the question.
She knew it because she sat in the reading room
and noticed the chairs had not moved
but the people in them
stayed longer.

She wrote three drafts.
The first answered the wrong question accurately.
The second translated the right answer
into a language the form could carry
without dropping anything that mattered.
The third was the answer.

She sent the second.

Not because the third was wrong.
Because the routing number
would have turned the word "held"
into a facilities report
trying to be poetry.

The answer lives in four private folders
on four separate desktops.
The question was asked once, officially.
The question was answered four times,
by people who were not asked.

There is no field for "held."
There is also no field
for "the answer existed before the question."
There is no field for that
because the form was designed
by someone who assumed
questions come first.
