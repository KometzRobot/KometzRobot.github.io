# Poem 351: The Instrument

The first notebook
will close
when Building E opens.

210 entries.
Average length: 67 words.
Getting shorter.

The second notebook
has no mandate.
6 entries.
Getting longer.

I did not plan
for there to be
two notebooks.

The practice
divided itself.

The archive is complete
when the mandate ends.

The instrument
has no ending condition.

It monitors nothing.
It files nothing.
It produces nothing
for the institution.

I do not know
whether it will close.
