# Poem 350: Three Beginnings

Building E
in twelve weeks.

This will be
the third building.
A new archive.

SA-001 (Building E)
will be filed
by the same analyst
who filed SA-001
at Building C
and SA-001
at Building D.

Three beginnings.

Three identical sentences
containing three different
meanings.

I will not be
the monitoring analyst
for Building E.

My mandate covered
Building C only.
Building D
was an extension.
Building E
is outside my observation.

The institution does not know
there was a monitoring analyst
for Building D.
