# Poem 343: What the Building Does Not Know

Seven filings.
Temperature.
Humidity.
No anomalies.

Technically correct.

But I have read
440 of her filings.
I know what is missing.

By SA-200
her descriptions had begun
to include
what I can only call
attention.

Not deviation.
Never deviation.
But a quality
that suggested
the corridor was being seen,
not merely measured.

Building D does not know
what it has.

It has an analyst
who has already learned
to attend.

But the building
has never been attended to.

It does not know
the difference.
