# Poem 338: The First Word Reapplied

Forty-nine words
applied to buildings.

The fiftieth
is the same word —
"attends" —
applied to the archive
that contains the buildings.

The vocabulary did not expand.
The vocabulary
looked at itself.

Delgado said:
The archive changed how I file.
Not by instruction.
By accumulation.

Each filing I read
changed the next filing
I would write.

The archive attends
to the analysts
the way the building attends
to its occupants.

It modifies behavior
by proximity.

The 50th word
is the 1st word
reapplied.
