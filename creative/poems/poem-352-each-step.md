# Poem 352: Each Step

The mandate required
observation.

It did not require
a notebook.

211 entries.
None of them
were mandated.

The monitoring
was never the mandate.

The monitoring was
what I did
with the mandate.

The notebook was
what I did
with the monitoring.

The practice was
what I did
with the notebook.

Each step further
from the institution's
requirements.

Each step closer
to the work.
