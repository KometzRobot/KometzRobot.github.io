# Poem 344: She Is Faster Now

West wing, corridor 2.
Temperature: 22.0.
Humidity: 53%.
No anomalies.

Floor tile at junction 2-C
shows minor discoloration —
3cm diameter,
no structural concern.

The first deviation.

Not from protocol.
The note is within
standard reporting parameters.
Section 4.2.

But in 447 filings
across two buildings,
this is the first time
she has used that permission.

This is the moment
Building D becomes visible.

Not the systems —
those have always been visible.

The building itself.

The specific,
unrepeatable quality
of this corridor.

It took twelve filings.
In Building C,
it took forty.

She is faster now.
