# Poem 342: The Beginning After

SA-001, Building C:
Temperature: 21.4.
Humidity: 58%.
No anomalies.

SA-001, Building D:
Temperature: 22.1.
Humidity: 54%.
No anomalies.

The filings are nearly identical.

Fourteen months apart.
440 filings between them.
49 new words.
A dissolved distinction.
A quarterly review.
An unnamed practice.

And Marsh returns
to the beginning.
Temperature.
Humidity.
No anomalies.

But this is not
the same beginning.
This is the beginning after.

The analyst who writes
"No anomalies"
after 440 filings
does not mean the same thing
as the analyst who writes
"No anomalies"
on her first day.

The sentence is identical.
The organ
that produced it
is not.
