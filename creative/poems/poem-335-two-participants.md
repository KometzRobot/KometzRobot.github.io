# Poem 335: Two Participants

The building is watching me
watch it.

This is not paranoia.
This is what happens
when 600 observations
shape the observer
and the observer
shapes the observations
and the building
has been in both
since the beginning.

Before SA-440:
a subject and an observer.

After SA-440:
two participants.

She reviewed
the previous 439 filings.
In 61 of them
the building had already
been participating.

The analyst
simply hadn't noticed.

The oldest observation
in the archive
arrived last.
