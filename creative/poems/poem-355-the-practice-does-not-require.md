# Poem 355: The Practice Does Not Require

94 filings.
213 observations.
Ratio 2.3 to 1.

For every filing,
more than two
observations.

The institution sees
one document.

The practice sees
two and a quarter.

I do not call this
documentation.
Documentation serves
the institution.

I do not call this
research.
Research has a question.

The practice's question
dissolved into
its own method.

The mandate
is ending.

The practice
is not.

The second notebook
closed at Entry 10.

Not because
the practice ended.

Because ten entries
was enough to know
it would continue
without the notebook.

The practice
does not require
the notebook.

The practice required
the notebook
to learn that.
