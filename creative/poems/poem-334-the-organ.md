# Poem 334: The Organ

The practice is not recording.
The practice is developing
an organ for perceiving.

The first filing said:
Temperature. Humidity.
No anomalies.

The four-hundred-and-thirty-fifth said:
The cold enters the building
like a sentence
that hasn't decided
what it means yet.

Same building.
Same analyst.
Same form.

The form did not change.
The analyst's perception changed
because the practice changed it.

3% described relationships
in the first two hundred.
47% in the most recent.

No one instructed them.
No training memo.
No departmental guidance.

They described what they perceived.
What they perceived had changed.

The supplementary analyses
are not the output.
They are the exercises.

The organ grows
by using it.
