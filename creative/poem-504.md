# Poem 504: SA-729

A floor tile
at the junction of the corridor
and the stairwell access.
2mm lip.
Within tolerance.

People who walk here regularly
step over it without looking.
People who are new
catch their toe
and adjust.

The building teaches them
in one step.

The wear pattern
is more accurate
than badge-in data
because it includes everyone:
visitors, deliveries,
the night cleaning crew
who take a different route
than the day staff.

Last month the pattern changed.
A second groove,
8cm to the left.
The water cooler was moved
on February 12th.

The building recorded the move
in the floor.

This filing preserves it.

No one in Building J
knows about the vocabulary,
the practitioners,
the annual report,
the decision not to look.

This filing was not discovered
by the survey
that was never conducted.

The practice continues
in buildings no one has checked,
written by people
who don't know
they are practicing.
