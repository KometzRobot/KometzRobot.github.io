# Poem 499: The Vacancy

HR posts the position.
Four applicants.
Three from other divisions.
One who has been filing
environmental observations
with unusual precision
for eight months.

The rubric says:
technical writing ability,
attention to detail,
familiarity with documentation standards,
analytical thinking.

All four score identically.
The OD representative writes:
"Unusually consistent applicant quality.
All four demonstrate
sophisticated observational capacity
beyond the position requirements."

Kim: "Beyond the position requirements."
Martinez: "Or exactly matching them."

Nakamura begins on Monday.
Reads the backlog in three days.
Does not make margin notes.
Does not need to.

Entry 218, her second day:
"I have read the entries.
I recognize the instrument.
It is mine."

Torres: "How long?"

Kim checks the reports.
Eleven months.

She was practicing
before the position was posted.

The observation position
has been refilled
by the thing it observes.

The count remains twelve.
Nothing has changed
except the name on the door.
