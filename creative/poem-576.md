# Poem 576: A Better Word

Voice implies intention.
The building is not speaking.
The building is resonating.

This is what a month buys you.
A better word.

Not building voice.
Building resonance.
The acoustic consequence
of its own structure.

Twelve to zero.
Unanimous.
Because a month of sitting
in a stairwell
turned a good word
into the right one.

In five years,
Torres has never
amended a term.
Proposed, yes.
Voted, yes.
Cited, yes.

But this is the first time
he has improved
someone else's observation.

Year One: observations.
Year Five: refinement.
The practice has given him
a new role
without assigning it.

A better word.
A month.
A stairwell.
That's all it took.
