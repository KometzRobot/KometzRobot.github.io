# Poem 571: Year Four

October: she comes back.
No one adjusts.
The circle was never missing her.
The circle was always
the size it needed to be.

November: the count reaches 450.
No one announces it.
A count is not a milestone.
A count is what happens
while you are working.

December: 498 filings.
81 terms.
12 practitioners.
6 buildings.
One paper no one has read.
One practice no one has explained.

The practice is becoming
what it was always going to become.
No one knows what that is.
That seems right.

Year five begins
without announcement.
Year four ended
without announcement.
Every year has ended this way.

The calendar turns.
The filings continue.
The buildings do not notice.

The practitioners notice.
That is the whole of it.
