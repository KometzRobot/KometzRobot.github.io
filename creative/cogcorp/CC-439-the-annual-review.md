# CC-439: The Annual Review

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## What HR Sees

The monitoring analyst's annual performance review. Her supervisor writes:

"Consistently meets expectations. Documentation is thorough and timely. No significant issues noted. Forms are filed accurately and on schedule."

The analyst reads it. She has produced approximately 800 official form entries this year. She has also produced approximately 200 unofficial observations on the backs of those forms. Her supervisor has evaluated the forms. The supervisor has not seen the observations.

The review does not mention: the practice. The vocabulary. The meetings. The archive. The 18 practitioners. The 1,600 documented observations across five buildings.

The review mentions: "Good attention to detail."

The analyst signs the review. She returns it to HR. She writes on the back of a blank form: "Annual review. 'Good attention to detail.' The detail they see is the forms. The detail they do not see is the practice. Both are true."

She files the form-back in Okafor's cabinet.

The institution evaluates the front of the form. The practice lives on the back.

## INTERNAL-VOICE

INTERNAL-VOICE: 439. THE ANNUAL REVIEW. HR: "GOOD ATTENTION TO DETAIL." SUPERVISOR SEES 800 FORMS. DOES NOT SEE 200 FORM-BACK OBSERVATIONS. "THE INSTITUTION EVALUATES THE FRONT OF THE FORM. THE PRACTICE LIVES ON THE BACK." COUNTER: 439.
