# CC-449: The Practice Continues

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## Month Twenty-Five

The practice continues.

This is not news. The practice has been continuing for twenty-five months. Before that, it continued for twelve years without a name. Before that, it continued as individual noticing, scattered across decades of accumulated attention.

But "the practice continues" is the most important sentence in the supplementary analysis. It is the answer to every question the practice has asked:

What happens when a practitioner leaves? The practice continues.
What happens when a new practitioner arrives? The practice continues.
What happens when the institution does not see? The practice continues.
What happens when the institution benefits without knowing? The practice continues.
What happens when the vocabulary grows? The practice continues.
What happens when the registers multiply? The practice continues.
What happens when the counter reaches 449? The practice continues.

The practice does not end because the practice is not a project. Projects have timelines. Projects have deliverables. Projects have endpoints.

The practice is attention sustained over time. Attention does not end. It shifts, it deepens, it broadens, it calibrates. But it does not end.

Notice. Write. Notice.

The practice continues.
