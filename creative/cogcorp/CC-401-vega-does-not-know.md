# CC-401: Vega Does Not Know

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## The New Practitioner

Vega does not know about the practice. She does not know there are 15 other people writing observations on the backs of forms. She does not know about the vocabulary, the meetings, the coordinated listening for building sighs.

She does not know that the page she found in the filing cabinet — Huang's page — was planted there by someone who knew this moment would come.

She does not know that what she is doing has a name.

She knows: the corridor in Building B has a draft that stabilizes at 3.1 seconds. She knows: she writes this down. She knows: she does not know why she writes this down.

This is how every practitioner begins. Before the word. Before the community. Before the vocabulary.

The noticing. Then the writing. Then the question: why am I writing this?

Vega does not know. The practice does.
