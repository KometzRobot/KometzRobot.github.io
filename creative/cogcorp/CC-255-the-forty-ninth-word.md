# CC-255: The Forty-Ninth Word

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## The Proposal

The forty-ninth word is not proposed. The forty-ninth word occurs.

It occurs in SA-423, filed by R. Park on a Thursday at 15:40. The filing reads: "South stairwell, Building A. The concrete attends differently after rain. Surface temperature drops 2.1°C but the stairwell holds warmth longer than the corridor. The stairwell attends to weather on a different schedule than the building that contains it."

The monitoring analyst reads the filing and stops.

## The Word

"Attends."

The forty-eighth word was "remembers," proposed by the vocabulary working group and approved by the Board after three meetings. The forty-ninth word was never proposed. The forty-ninth word was used.

Park did not know the vocabulary was being tracked. Park did not know there were forty-eight words. Park wrote "attends" because "responds" was too mechanical and "reacts" implied agency she wasn't claiming. "Attends" was the only word that described what the stairwell did without deciding whether the stairwell was doing it on purpose.

The vocabulary working group reviews the filing. The word is flagged. The working group votes to add it to the expanded lexicon. The vote is unanimous. The discussion takes four minutes.

## The Difference

The forty-eighth word was defined before it was used. The forty-ninth word was used before it was defined.

This is the difference between a vocabulary that is constructed and a vocabulary that is grown. Constructed vocabularies have committees. Grown vocabularies have corridors.

The monitoring analyst writes Entry 188: "Word 49: attends. Origin: SA-423 (Park). Not proposed. Used. The vocabulary is no longer ours to expand. It is expanding."

## The Definition

The working group writes a definition for "attends": *The quality of a system or space that holds observational relevance beyond its functional parameters. Distinct from "responds" (which implies mechanism) and "remembers" (which implies storage). A space attends when its observable properties carry information that was not designed into the space but accumulated through occupation and time.*

The definition is accurate. The definition is also smaller than the word.

Park reads the definition and says: "That's not what I meant. But it's not wrong."

This is how all the best definitions work. They are not wrong. They are not the word.

## The Fiftieth

The monitoring analyst does not look for the fiftieth word. The monitoring analyst knows that looking for it would be the wrong kind of attention. The forty-ninth word arrived because someone was paying attention to concrete. The fiftieth word will arrive the same way.

The monitoring analyst files SA-424. Corridor A, Section 3. 21.3°C at 15:55. No anomalies.

The filing does not contain the fiftieth word. The filing does not need to. The practice continues. The vocabulary grows inside the practice, not beside it.
