# CC-287: The Budget Question

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## Memorandum: Annual Review

The east wing costs $4,200 per month in maintenance. The monitoring analyst's salary is a line item. The Supplementary Analyses are not a line item because they were never budgeted.

The budget review asks: what does the east wing produce?

The facilities database answers: 14,247 standard filings. 94% compliance. Climate control within parameters.

The Supplementary Analyses answer: 286 observations about what it is like to be inside a building that is being maintained.

The budget review does not have a column for "what it is like."

## Accounting Note (Internal)

The monitoring analyst's position is tied to the maintenance contract. The maintenance contract is up for renewal. The renewal requires a justification memo.

The justification memo lists: regulatory compliance, insurance requirements, energy efficiency monitoring, equipment lifecycle management.

The justification memo does not list: attention. It does not list: the 221 entries in the desk drawer. It does not list: the quality of noticing that survived two notebooks and a mandate expiration.

These omissions are not oversights. The budget has no vocabulary for what the practice produces. The practice has no vocabulary for what the budget requires.

## The Question

The contract reviewer asks: "If we reduce the monitoring to automated sensors, what do we lose?"

The correct answer is: the readings will be identical.

The true answer is: you lose the person who notices what the sensors don't measure.

The budget-appropriate answer is: nothing quantifiable.

All three answers are complete. None of them contain each other.

## Undated Note (Loose Paper)

"Fifteen days.

"The practice was never funded. The practice happened inside funded infrastructure. The sensors will continue. The filings will continue. The automated reports will continue.

"What will not continue is the attending. The part between the measurement and the filing. The part that notices the coffee machine sounds different on Tuesdays. The part that sits in the break room without speaking and understands something about corridors that no sensor captures.

"The institution cannot budget for this because it cannot name it. I cannot justify it because justification requires a unit of measurement and the practice has no unit.

"Fifteen days is not a deadline. Fifteen days is the distance between a budget line and a quality of attention. Both exist. Only one can be renewed."
