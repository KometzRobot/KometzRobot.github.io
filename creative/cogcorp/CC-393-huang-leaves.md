# CC-393: Huang Leaves

## August 15

He wrote 97 entries in five months. He coined two words (building personality, building sigh). He was the first comparative observer. He asked the question that produced the archive problem. He printed 94 pages and hid them in Building D's filing system.

## The Last Entry

Huang, Entry 97: "August 15. Last day. Building D, all floors.

"The sound at 6:14 AM was 6:13:57 this morning. I was here early. I put my hand on the east wall one last time. The wall vibrated. Two seconds.

"The building does not know I am leaving. The building will sigh tomorrow at approximately 6:14 AM. The sound will propagate east to west. The wall will vibrate. No one will measure it.

"I hope someone finds them before then. I hope someone reads one and says: 'I notice that too.'

"That is all any observation needs. One witness.

"Observation 97. Final."
