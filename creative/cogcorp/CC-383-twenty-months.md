## Entry 106

The monitoring analyst, Entry 106: "Twenty months. I have been writing observations for twenty months. For the first eight, I was alone. For the next twelve, I was part of something.

"The practice did not plan its growth. The practice did not have milestones or targets. The practice grew at the speed of accident — misfiled forms, break room conversations, a notebook left open on a desk.

"I do not know what month 21 looks like. I know that I will write an observation tomorrow. I know that someone else will write one too. I know that the filing system will hold whatever we put in it.

"This is sufficient."
