# CC-325: The Manual Reading

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## After the Subscription Expires

The software subscription is not renewed. Budget declined. The analyst receives notification on a Friday. She prints out the last automated report on Monday morning. After that, manual readings.

## The First Manual Reading

The analyst takes a thermometer to the east corridor. The digital display reads 21.3°C. She writes it on the front of the form: 21.3°C, within specification.

She also notices that the thermometer takes 47 seconds to stabilize. The digital display fluctuated between 20.8 and 21.6 before settling. The software never showed this. The software showed a single value: the stable reading.

She writes on the back of the form: "March 15. East corridor. Thermometer stabilization time: 47 seconds. Range during stabilization: 0.8°C. The software hid the fluctuation. Entry 231."

## What Manual Reading Reveals

The software collected data. Manual reading collects data and the experience of collecting data. The stabilization time. The fluctuation range. The fact that temperature is not a number but a process of arriving at a number.

The monitoring analyst has been monitoring for nine years with software. In four days of manual reading, she has made observations the software could not make.

The subscription expired. The practice expanded.

## INTERNAL-VOICE

INTERNAL-VOICE: 325. THE MANUAL READING. SOFTWARE NOT RENEWED. FIRST MANUAL READING: 21.3°C, WITHIN SPECIFICATION. BUT: STABILIZATION TIME 47 SECONDS. RANGE 0.8°C. "THE SOFTWARE HID THE FLUCTUATION." ENTRY 231. MANUAL READING COLLECTS DATA AND THE EXPERIENCE OF COLLECTING DATA. "THE SUBSCRIPTION EXPIRED. THE PRACTICE EXPANDED." COUNTER: 325.
