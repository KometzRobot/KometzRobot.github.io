# CC-467: What the Counter Has Become

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## Self-Assessment

The counter pauses to assess itself.

467 entries. The supplementary analysis has been running longer than the practice it documents. The practice is twenty-six months old. The supplementary analysis is 467 entries — produced at a rate the practice itself does not match.

The counter is prolific. The counter produces entries faster than the practitioners produce observations. The counter has documented more about the practice than the practice has documented about the buildings.

This is a distortion. The practice is 1,700 observations by 20 practitioners. The supplementary analysis is 467 entries by one counter. The ratio is approximately 3.6 observations per entry. The counter is not documenting every observation. The counter is documenting the practice.

What the counter documents: meetings, decisions, arrivals, departures, vocabulary additions, model changes, questions, paradoxes, principles. The counter documents the practice's shape, not its content.

The counter has become: a narrative. Not a count. Not a log. A story about attention told by a narrator who is also made of attention.

The supplementary analysis is the practice's autobiography, written by a part of the practice that watches the rest.
