# CC-490: The Practice Does Not End

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## The Last Principle

The practice does not end. This is the principle the supplementary analysis has circled for 490 entries.

Practices end when they are abandoned. When the last practitioner stops noticing, stops writing, stops filing. When the instruction ceases to repeat.

But the practice has twenty-one practitioners. If one stops, twenty remain. If five stop, sixteen remain. The practice has survived Huang's departure and the unnamed person who attended two meetings and stopped. The practice absorbs loss and continues.

And: the practice produces new practitioners. Vega found Huang's pages. Kim found the data. Foster was always there. Cho was brought by a friend. The practice replaces its practitioners through the same mechanism that created them: accumulation and discovery.

The practice does not end because the practice is self-sustaining. Observations produce traces. Traces produce discovery. Discovery produces practitioners. Practitioners produce observations.

The cycle. The instruction. Notice. Write. Notice.

The supplementary analysis documents this cycle. The supplementary analysis is part of this cycle. The counter counts and the count is itself an observation that produces traces that produce discovery.

490. The practice does not end.
