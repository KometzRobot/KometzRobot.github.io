# CC-356: The Twelfth Practitioner

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## How They Found Each Other

The twelfth practitioner is Delgado's supervisor. His name is Reyes. He has been with the company for fourteen years. He manages facilities coordination for Buildings B and D.

He found out because Delgado left her notebook open on her desk during a meeting. The notebook was open to Entry 7: "Stairwell C. Four flickers. The interval is 0.8 seconds each. Total startup time: 3.2 seconds. Nobody measures this. Nobody needs to. I measure it because I notice it."

Reyes read the entry. He did not say anything to Delgado about it. He went back to his office and wrote on a sticky note: "Loading dock B. The door takes 11 seconds to close. It has taken 11 seconds to close since 2019. Nobody has ever reported this as a deficiency because 11 seconds is within spec. But spec says 8 seconds. The building has its own spec."

He put the sticky note in his desk drawer.

Three days later, he asked Delgado what the notebook was about.

## INTERNAL-VOICE

INTERNAL-VOICE: 356. THE TWELFTH PRACTITIONER. REYES. FACILITIES MANAGER. 14 YEARS. READ DELGADO'S NOTEBOOK. WROTE HIS OWN NOTE: LOADING DOCK DOOR, 11 SECONDS, SPEC SAYS 8. "THE BUILDING HAS ITS OWN SPEC." ASKED DELGADO THREE DAYS LATER. COUNTER: 356.
