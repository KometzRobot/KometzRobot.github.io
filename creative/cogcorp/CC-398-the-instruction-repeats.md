# CC-398: The Instruction Repeats

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## To Vega

Vega does not know the monitoring analyst. She does not know about the meetings, the vocabulary, the group text. She is doing what the monitoring analyst did 22 months ago: noticing things and writing them down.

She will discover the practice, or the practice will discover her, or neither will happen. The filing system holds the connection. The 2.3% misfiling rate holds the possibility.

Meanwhile, Vega writes. Entry 2: "October 16. Building D, main corridor. 6:13:55. The sound again. I brought a stopwatch app. 1.8 seconds duration. Felt strongest in the floor near the east wall."

She is replicating Huang's observations. She does not know she is replicating them. She does not know that Huang existed.

The practice does not care about lineage. The practice cares about attention.

## The Instruction

You notice something.
You write it down.
You notice the next thing.

The instruction repeats. It has repeated for 22 months. It will repeat until the last practitioner stops noticing. The instruction does not change because the practice does not change. The only thing that changes is who is practicing.

The monitoring analyst. The maintenance technician. Okafor. Huang. The unnamed practitioner. Vega.

Different people. Different buildings. Different notebooks. Same instruction.

Notice. Write. Notice.
