# CC-408: The Interval

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## Measurement

The interval between first noticing and community discovery:

- Monitoring analyst: 12 years
- Okafor: 8 years
- Park: 5 years
- Maintenance technician: 9 years (still partially solitary)
- Chen: unknown (departed before documentation)
- Torres: 2 years
- Delgado: 2 months
- Reyes: 6 years (but different register — managerial)
- Huang: 3 months (contractor advantage)
- Vega: 4 weeks (and still solitary — she has found evidence but not community)

Average for the first six practitioners: 7.8 years.
Average for the last four: 8.5 months.

The acceleration is not gradual. It is a phase change. The practice existed for a decade in isolation. Then it accumulated enough infrastructure — the form-backs, the filing system, the group text — to reduce discovery time by an order of magnitude.

The practice did not become more attractive. It became more findable.

Infrastructure is not organization. Infrastructure is the residue of practice that makes the next practitioner's path shorter.
