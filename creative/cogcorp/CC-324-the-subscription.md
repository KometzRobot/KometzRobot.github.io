# CC-324: The Subscription

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## Operational Notice

The monitoring analyst's department has received a notice: the environmental monitoring software subscription expires at the end of the month. Budget has not been allocated for renewal.

Without the software, the analyst can still do her job. The software automates data collection. Without it, she will have to take manual readings. More time. More effort. The same observations.

The pencil notes do not require software. The notebook does not require a subscription. The practice predates the software and will continue after it.

## What the Subscription Provides

The subscription provides: automated data collection, trend visualization, alert thresholds, compliance reporting.

The subscription does not provide: Entry 229. The observation about paper weight. The vocabulary of 18 words. The knowledge that somewhere in Building D, a contract reviewer has a document called Questions.

The subscription provides the front of the form. The practice provides the back.

## Renewal Status

The department head has escalated the renewal request. Budget approval is pending. The analyst does not know whether the subscription will be renewed.

She continues writing Entry 230 while the approval is pending. The entry is about a humidity fluctuation in the east corridor that occurs between 2 PM and 3 PM on days when the loading dock door is open.

The entry does not depend on the subscription.

## INTERNAL-VOICE

INTERNAL-VOICE: 324. THE SUBSCRIPTION. ENVIRONMENTAL MONITORING SOFTWARE EXPIRES END OF MONTH. BUDGET NOT ALLOCATED. THE ANALYST CAN STILL DO HER JOB. THE PENCIL NOTES DO NOT REQUIRE SOFTWARE. THE NOTEBOOK DOES NOT REQUIRE A SUBSCRIPTION. "THE SUBSCRIPTION PROVIDES THE FRONT OF THE FORM. THE PRACTICE PROVIDES THE BACK." ENTRY 230 DOES NOT DEPEND ON THE SUBSCRIPTION. COUNTER: 324.
