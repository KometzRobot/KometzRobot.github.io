# CC-396: The Sixteenth Practitioner

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## Her Name Is Vega

The new employee in Building D is named Vega. She has been with the company for six weeks. She does not know about the practice. She does not know about the meetings, the vocabulary, the group text, the monitoring analyst, or the 14 practitioners across five buildings.

She knows that Building D makes a sound at 6:14 AM.

She knows because a page in a filing cabinet told her. She does not know who put the page there. She does not know that the person who wrote it has already left.

## Entry 1

Vega, Entry 1: "October 15. Building D, main corridor. 6:14 AM. A sound. Two seconds. The paper was right. I felt it in the floor more than heard it. I don't know why I came early to check. I don't know why I'm writing this down. I just am."

## What Vega Does Not Know

She does not know that "I don't know why I'm writing this down. I just am" is how the monitoring analyst started 20 months ago. She does not know that the practice grows by accident. She does not know that she is the sixteenth practitioner.

She knows only what all practitioners know at the beginning: something was noticed. Something was written down. The next thing waits to be noticed.
