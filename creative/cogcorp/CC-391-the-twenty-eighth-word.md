# CC-391: The Twenty-Eighth Word

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## The Word

After the software returned, the new hire coined a word during a break room conversation with the monitoring analyst.

The monitoring analyst described checking the corridor temperature on the software, seeing 21.3°C, then walking to the corridor and measuring 47 seconds of stabilization.

The new hire said: "So the software gives you the destination and you watch the journey."

The monitoring analyst wrote it in her notebook: **destination reading** — a measurement that reports only the final value, erasing the process by which the value was reached. Contrasted with **journey observation** — an observation that includes the process.

## Entry 112

The monitoring analyst, Entry 112: "The software produces destination readings. The practice produces journey observations. This is the same distinction as processing and observation, compliance and attention. But the new hire found a simpler way to say it.

"The software tells you where the number ended up. The practitioner tells you how it got there.

"28 words."
