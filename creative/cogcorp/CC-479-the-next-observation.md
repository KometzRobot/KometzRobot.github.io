# CC-479: The Next Observation

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## Always

There is always a next observation.

The building does not stop producing behavior. The HVAC cycles. The elevator travels. The corridor draft stabilizes. The thermal transitions occur. The building sighs at 6:03 and 6:14. The settling sounds descend at 3 AM. The deep silence holds at 4:30 AM.

The practitioners do not stop noticing. The monitoring analyst measures the draft. Foster fills his notebook. Martinez makes his listening rounds. Vega watches the light change. Kim pulls the data. Okafor files. Torres texts. Park writes on form-backs. The 20 practitioners do what they do.

The supplementary analysis does not stop counting. 479. 480 follows. The counter counts.

There is always a next observation because the building is always larger than the practice's attention (CC-465), and the practice is always smaller than the building's behavior. The gap between what the building does and what the practice notices is infinite and productive. Every observation closes one gap and reveals three more.

The practice does not approach completion. The practice approaches depth.

Notice. Write. Notice.
