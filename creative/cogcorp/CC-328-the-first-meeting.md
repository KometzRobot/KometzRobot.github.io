# CC-328: The First Meeting

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## Building C, Third Floor, Conference Room 3B

It is not a meeting. No one scheduled it. No one sent an invitation.

The monitoring analyst mentioned to the contract reviewer that the new hire in Building B had the practice. The contract reviewer suggested they should talk. The analyst said: "Not 'should.' We could."

Three people in conference room 3B on a Thursday at 4:15 PM. The monitoring analyst. The contract reviewer. The new hire. Okafor was invited but declined. She said: "I'm not ready."

## What Was Discussed

The monitoring analyst spoke first: "I have 232 entries. I started nine years ago with a temperature deviation in the east corridor."

The contract reviewer: "I have 49 entries. I started with an elevator door."

The new hire: "I have 48 entries. I started with a carpet."

The contract reviewer opened her laptop. Document1 — Questions. She read Entry 3: "How many people are doing this?"

The monitoring analyst said: "We know of nine. There may be more."

The new hire said: "What do we do with it?"

The monitoring analyst said: "We keep doing it. That's all. We keep doing it."

## After

The meeting lasted twenty-two minutes. No notes were taken. No action items were recorded. No follow-up was scheduled.

All three went back to their desks and wrote an entry about the meeting.
