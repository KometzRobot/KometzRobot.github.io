# CC-307: The New Hire

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## Building B, Ground Floor

The new hire starts on a Monday. She is assigned to environmental monitoring — humidity, air quality, temperature variance. Her desk is near the window. She has a clipboard and a login.

She fills out her first report by noon. Temperature: within specification. Humidity: within specification. Air quality: within specification.

At 2:47 PM, she notices the carpet in the east corridor has a slight discoloration near the third support column. It is not on her form. There is no field for it.

She notes it anyway. On the back of the form. In pencil.

## Week Two

The new hire has seven pencil notes on the backs of six forms. The discoloration. A draft that appears between 3:15 and 3:40 PM only. A light that takes 1.1 seconds to reach full brightness. A sound from the ventilation that occurs once per hour, for four seconds, at irregular intervals.

None of these appear on the front of any form.

She has not told anyone about the pencil notes. She does not know there is anyone to tell.
