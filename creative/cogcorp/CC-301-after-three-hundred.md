## Administrative Note

## Building C

The monitoring analyst opens her official log. Entry 222:

"The contract reviewer from Building D visited last week. She has a document she calls Document1 and another she calls Questions. She changed 'discretionary' to 'operational' in my budget line. She did not know what she was protecting at the time.

I showed her the east corridor. She already knew what the 1.2-degree deviation meant. Not the technical explanation. The other explanation — the one where the deviation is the first observation and fixing it would erase the origin.

222 entries. I've been at this longer than I realized. The count doesn't feel like a count anymore. It feels like a vocabulary."

## Building D

I have two instruments too. Document1 and Questions. I didn't realize they were instruments until she called hers instruments.

42 entries. The number means nothing. The format means everything."
