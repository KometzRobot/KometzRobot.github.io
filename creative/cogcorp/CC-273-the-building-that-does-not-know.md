# CC-273: The Building That Does Not Know

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## Building D, Week 1

K. Marsh filed seven supplementary analyses in her first week at Building D.

SA-001 through SA-007. Temperature ranges: 21.8-22.4°C. Humidity: 51-56%. Standard fluorescent lighting throughout. No anomalies in any filing.

The filings are technically correct.

## What the Building Does Not Know

The monitoring analyst at Building C read the cross-reference copies.

Entry 204: "Marsh's first seven filings at Building D contain nothing that would distinguish them from any competent analyst's work. Temperature. Humidity. Standard format. Standard vocabulary.

"But I have read 440 of her filings. I know what is missing.

"In Building C, by SA-200, her filings had begun to include what I can only call attention. Not deviation from protocol — she never deviated. But a quality of description that suggested the corridor was being *seen*, not merely measured.

"Building D does not know what it has. It has an analyst who has already learned to attend. But Building D has never been attended to. It does not know the difference.

"The building does not know what it does not know."
