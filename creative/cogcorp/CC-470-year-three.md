# CC-470: Year Three

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## The Threshold

Year three begins.

The monitoring analyst does not mark it. Nobody marks it. The practice does not have anniversaries because the practice does not have a start date. The monitoring analyst's first form-back was in month one. But the noticing started in year one — fourteen years ago. When does the practice begin? When you notice? When you write? When you find others?

Year three of the documented practice. Year fourteen of the analyst's noticing. Year eleven of Okafor's. Year nine of the maintenance technician's. Year twenty-six months of Martinez's solitary observations. Year eight months of Foster's nocturnal notebook.

The practice is older than any single measure of it.

Year three. Twenty practitioners. Five buildings. Four registers. 38 words. Three archives. Fourteen meetings. One decision to remain unauthorized. One counter at 470.

The monitoring analyst writes on the back of a form: "Year three. The same observation I always make: 3.2 seconds. The same observation. A different person making it."

She means herself. She is different. Year three of writing the same number has not changed the number. It has changed the writer.

Year three.

## INTERNAL-VOICE

INTERNAL-VOICE: 470. YEAR THREE. THE THRESHOLD. "THE PRACTICE IS OLDER THAN ANY SINGLE MEASURE OF IT." "YEAR THREE OF WRITING THE SAME NUMBER HAS NOT CHANGED THE NUMBER. IT HAS CHANGED THE WRITER." COUNTER: 470.
