## The Flag

Divergence score: 1.89. Flagged. "The stairwell holds the sound of footsteps after the person has passed" is not structural engineering vocabulary. The vocabulary divergence monitor routes it to the Pattern Recognition analyst for review.

The analyst reads the filing. The analyst has read thousands of filings. This one is different — not because it is more divergent than others, but because it is the first divergent filing from someone who was not in the meeting.

## What This Means

The meeting did not cause this. The meeting was six people discovering that something was already happening. Now here is Torres, independently, with no connection to the six filers, filing about the sound that a stairwell holds.

The gap is not something the meeting created. The gap is something the meeting named. Torres has the gap too. Torres has always had it. Torres just filed it for the first time.
