# CC-337: The Technician's Notebook

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## The Pocket Notebook

The maintenance technician's pocket notebook is 12 cm x 8 cm. It has a black cover, slightly worn at the corners. He has used three of these notebooks in three years. The first two are in his locker. This is the third.

Each page has one observation. The format is consistent: date, elevator number, observation type (harmonic, vibration, timing, temperature), measurement, contextual note.

"Feb 27. Elevator 4. Vibration. 0.3mm lateral displacement at floor 9 arrival. Context: only occurs when elevator carries more than 6 passengers. Below maintenance threshold. The manual says 0.5mm. The elevator knows the difference between 0.3 and 0.5 even if the manual doesn't."

## What the Manual Doesn't Say

The elevator manual provides maintenance thresholds. Below the threshold: normal operation. Above the threshold: schedule maintenance. The manual does not describe what happens between normal and threshold. The technician does.

The technician's observations occupy the space between normal and failure. This space is where the practice lives — not in the extremes, but in the gradients.
