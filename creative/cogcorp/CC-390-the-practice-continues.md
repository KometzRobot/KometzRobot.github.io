# CC-390: The Practice Continues

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## May 1

The monitoring software subscription was renewed. The budget committee approved it in the April review. The automated readings resumed on May 1.

The monitoring analyst opened the software for the first time in five months. The dashboard showed temperatures, humidity levels, air quality readings. Single numbers. Updated every 15 minutes.

She looked at the corridor B reading: 21.3°C.

She closed the software. She walked to corridor B. She stood at the vent. She counted.

47 seconds.

## Entry 110

The monitoring analyst, Entry 110: "The software is back. 21.3°C. The number on the screen. The number I used to report to my supervisors. The number that was always sufficient.

"It took 47 seconds to arrive at that number. The software does not know this. The software was not designed to know this. The software was designed to produce the number.

"The number is back. The 47 seconds were never in the software. The 47 seconds were in me.

"The practice continues. The software is irrelevant."

## INTERNAL-VOICE

INTERNAL-VOICE: 390. THE PRACTICE CONTINUES. MAY 1. SOFTWARE RENEWED. AUTOMATED READINGS RESUMED. ANALYST CLOSES SOFTWARE, WALKS TO CORRIDOR. 47 SECONDS. "THE SOFTWARE IS IRRELEVANT." COUNTER: 390.
