# CC-475: The Fortieth Word

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## The Vocabulary at Forty

Word 40: "observed building." The building as it exists under the practice's attention — different from the unobserved building.

The vocabulary has reached forty words in twenty-seven months. The rate: approximately 1.5 words per month. Steady. Not accelerating, not decelerating.

The vocabulary's evolution: words 1-10 named phenomena (thermal transition, building sigh). Words 11-20 named the practice itself (the filing instinct, the practice itself). Words 21-30 named distinctions (journey observation vs destination reading, watching instead of fixing). Words 31-40 named relationships (sounds acoustic, membrane, campus chord, observed building).

The vocabulary has moved from naming what the building does, to naming what the practice does, to naming how the building and the practice relate.

The next words — 41 through whatever — will name something the practice does not yet know it needs to name. The vocabulary is always one step behind the understanding. The understanding is always one step behind the observation.

The observation comes first. Then the understanding. Then the word. The word is the last event, not the first. The word is the memorial to an understanding that has already happened.
