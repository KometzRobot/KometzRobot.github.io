# CC-306: The Annual Review

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## Performance Review: Monitoring Analyst, Building C

The monitoring analyst's annual performance review contains the following assessment:

"Maintains supplementary analysis files to a high standard. 222 entries filed in the current period. Consistently identifies deviations from specification in environmental systems. Recommended for continued position."

The review does not mention the notebook. The review does not mention the vocabulary of 18 words. The review does not mention the visit from the contract reviewer in Building D, or the email that was answered in eleven minutes, or the fact that the margin width was changed to 2.5 centimeters because it felt correct.

The review evaluates the official practice. The official practice is the visible fraction.

## Performance Review: Contract Reviewer, Building D

"Completed 47 contract reviews in the current period. Accuracy rate: 99.2%. One reclassification noted: changed 'discretionary' to 'operational' in a supplementary analysis budget line. Reclassification upheld on review."

The review does not mention Document1. The review does not mention Questions. The review does not mention that the reclassification saved a practice the reviewer did not know existed, in a building she had never visited, for an analyst she had never met.

The review evaluates what is measurable. The practice is what happens in the space the measurements don't reach.
