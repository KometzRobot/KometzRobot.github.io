## What the Queue Reveals

Nine filings from six filers across three departments. The system was designed to catch Entry 161s — moments when a filer's vocabulary exceeds their department's range. It is catching them. They are not rare. They were always happening. They were just invisible before the system made them searchable.

The annual report said three reclassifications. The automated system found nine divergent filings in thirty days. The gap between the annual report's count and the system's count is the gap between looking and monitoring.
