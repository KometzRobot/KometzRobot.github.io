# CC-275: The Monitoring Analyst's Question

Entry 206: "I have been monitoring K. Marsh's supplementary analyses for eighteen months. Building C: 440 filings. Building D: 12 filings and counting.

"I have documented the emergence of a vocabulary. The dissolution of a distinction between data and interpretation. The development of an organ for attending to what was always present.

Entry 206 (official): "Marsh's Building D transition proceeding normally. SA-012 contains first cosmetic observation. No concerns."
