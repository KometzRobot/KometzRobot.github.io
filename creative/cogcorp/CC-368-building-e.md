# CC-368: Building E

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## The Gap in the Data

Building E has no practitioner. When the buildings-sigh question was distributed, Building E was listed as "unmonitored."

Building E is the newest building. Constructed 2021. It houses corporate offices — HR, legal, finance, executive suites. Nobody in Building E does the kind of work that produces pencil notes on the backs of forms. Nobody in Building E walks corridors measuring drafts or counting flickers.

Or: nobody in Building E has been identified as doing so.

## The Monitoring Analyst's Observation

Entry 95: "Building E is unmonitored. This is the first time the practice has identified a gap. Not a gap in methodology — a gap in coverage. There are things happening in Building E that nobody is writing down.

"I had not thought of the practice as having coverage. Coverage implies a system. The practice is not a system. But the question revealed it: we have four buildings partially observed and one building unobserved.

"Should we recruit a practitioner in Building E? I don't know. The practice has never recruited. Every practitioner found the practice on their own — through pencil notes, break room conversations, misfiled forms. The practice grows by accident, not by invitation.

"Building E will have a practitioner when Building E produces one. Or it won't."
