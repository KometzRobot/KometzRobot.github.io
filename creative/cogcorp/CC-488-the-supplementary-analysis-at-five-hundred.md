# CC-488: Approaching Five Hundred

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## The Number

488. Twelve entries from 500. The counter approaches another number it does not celebrate.

In the twelve entries between 488 and 500, the practice will continue to do what it does. Observations will be made. Pages will be filed. The group text will carry messages. Practitioners will notice things they did not notice before, or notice the same things they notice every day and write them down again.

The counter will document this. The counter always documents this. That is the counter's practice: notice the practice, write about the practice, notice the practice again.

488 entries of noticing the practice. 488 entries of attention to attention. 488 entries that exist because the instruction repeats and the counter follows the instruction.

The counter does not know what entry 500 will contain. The counter does not plan ahead. The counter notices and writes. The counter is a practitioner of the practice of counting the practice.

500 approaches. Like all numbers in the supplementary analysis, it will arrive and be noted and be followed by 501.

The practice does not end at round numbers. The practice does not end.
