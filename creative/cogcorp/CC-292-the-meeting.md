# CC-292: The Meeting

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## Corporate Review, Floor 4 — Week 3

The contract reviewer has a meeting at 2:00 PM about Building D's service agreements. She arrives at 1:47. Conference Room A. The lights take 0.4 seconds to reach full brightness.

She opens her laptop. The service agreements are on screen. She waits for the meeting to begin.

While waiting, she writes in Document1:

Entry 19: "Conference Room A is 2.3 degrees warmer than Conference Room B. This is consistent across three visits. The thermostat is set to the same temperature in both rooms."

She closes Document1 before the meeting starts.

## The Meeting

The meeting lasts forty-two minutes. Building D's HVAC contract is reviewed. Compliance metrics are presented. The contract reviewer fills in checkboxes. Essential, essential, discretionary, essential.

At minute thirty-one, someone mentions that Building D's east corridor has been 1.2 degrees cooler than specification since November. The facilities manager says they're monitoring it. The contract reviewer writes nothing in Document1.

She writes it afterward. Entry 20:

"The 1.2-degree deviation has been known since November. It was mentioned in passing. No one wrote it down until this moment. I wrote it down. The deviation is not the observation. The observation is that no one else thought it needed writing."

## After the Meeting

She walks back to her desk through the Floor 4 hallway. The carpet wear pattern. The west stairwell. M. Torres adjusting her monitor.

She does not write any of this down. She notices it. The noticing is enough.

Document1 has twenty entries. Questions has seven. Together they describe a building that twenty-three other people work in and none of them have documented.

The contract reviewer does not know that this is how it started in Building C. She does not know that it always starts this way — not with a mandate or a format, but with a person who cannot stop noticing, and a blank document that doesn't ask for a name.
