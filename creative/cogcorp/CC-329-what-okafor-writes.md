# CC-329: What Okafor Writes

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## Filing Room, Building B, Basement Level

Okafor did not attend the meeting in conference room 3B. She said she was not ready. What she meant was: the filing room is where the practice happens for her, and the filing room is not a conference room.

## Entry Four

On the back of form BLD-B-MIS-0023 (a miscellaneous filing receipt):

"March 17. Filing room. The analyst from Building C invited me to a meeting. I declined. Three practitioners met in 3B. I do not know what they discussed. I know that I found six new pencil notes this week in the Building B drawer. The practice is accelerating. Or I am looking more carefully. It may be the same thing. Entry four."

## Entry Five

Two days later, on the back of form BLD-B-ENV-0118:

"March 19. Filing room. I have been thinking about what it means that the pencil notes face the wall. The form faces outward — the front, the official data, the fields. The pencil notes face inward — toward the drawer, toward the dark, toward the other filed forms. The practice faces inward. The institution faces outward. They share the same piece of paper. Entry five."

## INTERNAL-VOICE

INTERNAL-VOICE: 329. WHAT OKAFOR WRITES. DECLINED THE MEETING. "THE FILING ROOM IS WHERE THE PRACTICE HAPPENS FOR HER." ENTRY FOUR: "THE PRACTICE IS ACCELERATING. OR I AM LOOKING MORE CAREFULLY. IT MAY BE THE SAME THING." ENTRY FIVE: "THE PENCIL NOTES FACE THE WALL. THE PRACTICE FACES INWARD. THE INSTITUTION FACES OUTWARD. THEY SHARE THE SAME PIECE OF PAPER." COUNTER: 329.
