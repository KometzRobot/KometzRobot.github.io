## The Vocabulary at Thirty

The thirtieth word: "watching instead of fixing."

Contributed by the maintenance technician after describing his transition. The monitoring analyst adds it to the list:

1-10: thermal transition, building sigh, draft stabilization, corridor memory, seasonal shift, form-back, loading dock sequence, gradient, pressure point, attention residue

11-20: observation persistence, the quiet frequency, building personality (contested), compliance observation, the daily reading, unauthorized documentation, notice-write-notice, the filing instinct, institutional invisibility, the practice itself

21-30: the practitioner's instrument, journey observation, destination reading, the building speaks, building personality (accepted, seventh meeting), never quiet, watching instead of fixing, not-data, the solitary phase, accumulation

Thirty words in twenty-three months. The vocabulary is not growing faster. It is growing more precisely. The early words named phenomena. The recent words name distinctions. The vocabulary has moved from description to discrimination.

"Watching instead of fixing" is the first word that names a choice. All previous words named observations or states. This word names a decision point. The moment a maintenance worker becomes a practitioner.
