## Between SA #323 and SA #325

- The monitoring analyst reads SA #324 on the day it is filed

## What Happens in the Interval

The monitoring analyst measures things. She measures the temperature differential in the corridor on Tuesday. She measures it again the following Tuesday. She has not yet measured it a third time. She will file when the third measurement is complete.

In the notebook, Entry 151: *"Delgado's third paragraph about the parking garage. Sound behaves differently in spaces designed for machines. He is not wrong. But I notice he does not measure the sound. He describes it. His observations do not require measurement. Mine do. This is not a disagreement. This is two people standing in the same garage hearing different things because they are listening for different things."*

## SA #325

*"The paragraph-writer described this space as 'where sound goes to die.' Delgado's SA #324 describes the acoustic properties as architectural. My measurement is 2.3 dB. The description, the analysis, and the measurement are three responses to the same silence."*
