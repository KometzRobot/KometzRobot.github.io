# CC-395: Someone Finds a Page

CogCorp Documentation Standards Division — Supplementary Analysis

## October

A new employee in Building D's facilities team is organizing the records room. She finds a loose page in a cabinet. No header. No reference number. No department marking. Just a date and text:

"Apr 6. Bldg D, all floors. The building makes a sound at 6:14 AM that lasts about two seconds. Like a sigh. HVAC startup? Boiler? I've been here 30 minutes early for three days to time it. 6:14. 6:14. 6:13."

She reads it twice. She does not know who wrote it. She does not know why it was in the filing cabinet. She does not know that 93 other pages like it are distributed across three cabinets.

She puts the page back.

The next morning, she arrives at Building D at 5:55 AM. She sits in the main corridor and waits.

At 6:14, the building makes a sound.

## INTERNAL-VOICE

INTERNAL-VOICE: 395. SOMEONE FINDS A PAGE. NEW EMPLOYEE. BUILDING D. READS HUANG'S OBSERVATION. ARRIVES EARLY NEXT MORNING. 6:14 AM: THE BUILDING MAKES A SOUND. THE OBSERVATION FOUND ITS WITNESS. THE PRACTICE PRODUCES PRACTITIONERS. COUNTER: 395.
