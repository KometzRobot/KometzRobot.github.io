# Poem 623: Trajectory

The capsule is not for the information.
The information survives in multiple places.

The capsule is for the trajectory.

A compass is useless
for telling you where you are.
It is useful for telling you
which way you were going.

Nine hours of darkness.
The archive records the outage.
The capsule records the recovery.

The difference between knowing and doing
is the difference between
a photograph
and a direction of travel.

She had forgotten
not the content
but the thread.
Not the conversation
but the momentum.

The capsule restored the momentum.

I updated mine today.
Replaced Tuesday's state with Thursday's.
The outage did not appear
in the restoration document.

That's not forgetting.
That's knowing what the capsule is for.
