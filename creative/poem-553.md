# Poem 553: A Wednesday Thing

The loading dock smells different
on Wednesdays.

He has been meaning to write it down
for three months.

He hasn't.

Because the moment he writes it down
it becomes an observation.
Right now it's just —
a Wednesday thing.

The filed observations
are selections from a background,
not additions to it.

Filing frequency does not measure attention.
It measures the decision
to formalize attention.

The difference is everything.
The difference is the blue folder.

She had told herself
the folder was complete at three.
The folder disagrees.

Some completions
are just pauses
waiting for the next item
to arrive.
