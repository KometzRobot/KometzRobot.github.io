# Poem 508: The Visit

She brings coffee.
Two cups.
Not an inspection.

The fire door
between 3A and 3B
is propped open again.
Someone found a new doorstop
within a week.
No work order.
No request.

"The filing describes
the absence.
The return
was never filed.
It just happened."

"Is that unusual?"

"You're using my word now."

The numbers are steady.
Underneath them
something is growing.
Not the practitioners.
The building itself.
The way people
relate to it.

Someone noticed
the door was closed
and decided the corridor
should be one space.

That is not in any filing.

Kim comes back
every month.
Never on the same day.
Never announced.

She never brings questions.
She brings coffee.
