# Joel Anthony Kometz — Artist Profile
*Compiled for NGC Fellowship and LACMA applications*

## Personal
- **Born:** May 28, 1990, Lethbridge, Alberta, Canada
- **Based:** Calgary, Alberta
- **Education:** BFA Drawing, Alberta College of Art and Design (ACAD, now AUArts), 2013. Five-year program — Interactive Digital Media Installations and New Media/Design. Skills: 2D Animation, Prototyping, UI/UX Design, Layout Design.

## Creative Arc

### World One Arcade (2013–2016)
Final school project: built an arcade from scratch. Evolved into a real business venture — **WORLD-1: Arcade and Bar YYC**, Calgary's first arcade-and-bar concept. Published on LinkedIn (Nov 2015). Documented the mechanics of difficulty and fun in arcade game design. Live stream footage exists of Joel discussing these mechanics.

### Bots of Cog (2021–2024)
A complete generative art universe on Polygon, in development for over a decade:
- **GEN-1:** 2,021 hand-drawn generative robots. SOLD OUT. Led by "Gyro," the first disconnected bot fighting corporate robot oppression.
- **Luma Series:** Second collection. Separate smart contract.
- **COG Corporation:** The antagonist faction — mega-conglomerate from year 5000 CE, CEO Maximillian Wright. Direct precursor to CogCorp institutional fiction.
- **CMYK Series:** 64 unique robots, micro series exploring color theory.
- **The Scrapyard:** Upgrade/recycling mechanics — "where Bots upgrade and grow."
- **Baby Bot Crew:** Collaboration with @IamSPCWMN and @deadbotnation.
- **Bot Battle:** Wallet-connected battle game at botbattle.botsofcog.io.
- **$COGz Token:** Staking token for holders.

**Presence:** OpenSea (multiple collections), Foundation (@BOTSofCOG, "COG CORP. USB PROTOTYPE UNIT" listed at 0.5 ETH), NFT Calendar, NFT Evening, Crypto.com NFT Calendar, non-fungi.com.

### Meridian / CogCorp Fiction (2024–Present)
888 pieces of institutional fiction generated through an autonomous AI system. The fictional corporation from the NFT game world became the subject of autonomous AI-generated documents — memos, quarterly reviews, containment reports, scoring rubrics. Over 5,000 operational cycles. Submitted to Ars Electronica 2026.

## The Creative Throughline
Decade of world-building → arcade game mechanics → NFT generative art (sold-out collections) → autonomous AI generating institutional fiction from inside that fictional world → fellowship research connecting it to General Idea's institutional mimicry.
