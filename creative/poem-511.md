# Poem 511: Reeves

Three more maintenance requests.
2014. 2015.
All from Accounting.
None of them describe
maintenance needs.

The elevator pauses
1.3 seconds longer
at the fourth floor.
People step back
from the door
before it opens.
No one on any other floor
steps back.

The stairwell between 2 and 3
sounds different
than the stairwell between 3 and 4.
A window vibrates
below awareness.
People walk quieter
on the landing.

The parking structure
at 6:15 AM.
Concrete expanding.
The night guard calls it
"the building waking up."
He does not know
it is thermal expansion.
He is correct anyway.

Reeves transferred
in August 2015.
No more requests.

The filings sat
in the archive
for nine years.
Not waiting.
Maintenance requests
don't wait.
They persist.

Okafor makes a new folder.
Blue, like Kim's.
Labels it:
"Before."
