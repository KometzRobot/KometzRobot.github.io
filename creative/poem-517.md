# Poem 517: After

The office is quieter
without Priya.
Not in volume.
In the way a room is quieter
when someone organized
has left it.

Page twelve:
vocabulary emergence.
Growth in bursts.
Three terms. Silence.
Seven in a week. Plateau.

The vocabulary expands
when someone encounters
what existing words
cannot describe.
It contracts into silence
when the words are sufficient.

Question 50:
"When did the vocabulary
stop being enough?"

She writes the answer
below the question —
something she has not done
since forty-four.

"The vocabulary stopped
being enough
the moment it started
being sufficient.
Sufficiency creates the boundary.
If the words work,
you use them.
If they don't,
you stay silent.
The silence is the edge."

Outside:
a pipe clicks.
The heating system transitions.

The sound has been here
longer than the vocabulary,
longer than the monitoring position,
longer than any of them.
