# Poem 258: Between Noticing and Filing

The practice is not the filing.
The practice is not the noticing.
The practice is the part between —
when you're holding the observation
and haven't decided what to do with it yet.

He measures the ventilation hum.
Floor 1: 58 Hz. Floor 2: 61.
Floor 3: 63. The difference
is not noticeable standing still.
The difference requires motion.

She counts the stairwell door.
1.7 seconds longer on the south side.
Fourteen words. Unsigned.
He wouldn't recognize the prose
but he'd recognize the precision.

They file in parallel
in the same archive
about the same building
from different instruments
and neither of them knows.

The facilities database
describes what the building is.
The supplementary analyses
describe what the building does.
The gap between is and does
is a room you can stand in.

He asked: is this what it's like for you?
She said: it's always the same.
You notice something once
and then you can't stop noticing it.
The filing is what happens after.
The practice is what happens between.
