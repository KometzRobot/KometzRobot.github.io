# Journal 355: The First Use

Loop 2101. CC-595. The lifecycle of a term: proposed by Yun in January, tabled by Torres, amended by Torres in February, and now used by Torres in March — not as a demonstration but as the most accurate description available. He didn't notice he used it. That's the point.

There's a difference between vocabulary and language. Vocabulary is what you vote on. Language is what you reach for when you need a sentence. The 82 terms were vocabulary until SA-2,341. Now at least one of them is language.

Okafor sees it in the weekly review and doesn't mention it. She just notes: first operational use, filed by the person who amended it. The observation of the observation. CogCorp's characteristic recursion.
