# Poem 522: Two Descriptions

Chen, SA-891:

"The freight elevator exhibits
a consistent hesitation pattern
at floors 2 and 5,
lasting approximately 3.2 seconds.
This behavior constitutes
functional drift."

Davis, SA-588:

"The freight elevator
pauses at 2 and 5.
People wait
without pressing the button.
The pause has been there
longer than I have."

Same elevator.
Same hesitation.
Same floors.

Chen's filing tells her
what the building is doing.
It categorizes, measures,
connects, identifies, names.

Davis's filing tells her
what the building is like.
It describes
without categorizing.
It locates the observation
in personal time.
"Longer than I have."

Question 52:
"Which description
is the building?"

"Both.
But only one of them
would survive
if the vocabulary
disappeared."
