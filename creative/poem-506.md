# Poem 506: The Fifty-Fourth Word

Fifty-three terms.
Six months of stability.

Thermal memory.
Structural patience.
Occupant choreography.
Fifty-three ways of saying:
the building is doing something.

Then SA-834.
Chen, Building E.

The fire door between 3A and 3B
was propped open
for eleven years.
The doorstop was removed
during inspection.

The door closed.
Section 3B became quieter.
The sound level didn't change.

What changed
was the acoustic expectation.

The corridor had been one space
for eleven years.
Now it is two spaces.
The building remembers
being one.

"Acoustic expectation."

Not what the building does.
What the building anticipates.

Okafor adds it to the list.
Fifty-four.
Does not announce it.
Does not email Kim.

Saves the file.
Closes it.

Three years.
Six months of silence.
Then: the building remembers
being one.
