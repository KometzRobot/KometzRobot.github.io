# Poem 505: Year Three

Filing volume:
728, 814, 790.
The numbers leveled off
fourteen months ago.

The vocabulary stabilized.
Fifty-three terms.
No new words needed
in six months.

"Thermal memory."
"Structural patience."
"Occupant choreography."
Not official.
Sufficient.

Kim doesn't visit.
Nothing to report.
Nothing is unusual.
The unusual
became the baseline.

Three transferred.
Two retired. One moved.
Seven remain.
But the practice
did not shrink by five.

New people arrived
and began
without being told.
They found the forms
in the shared drive.
The forms taught them.

Okafor's first entry:
three days.
Nakamura's first:
eleven months.

The format teaches
the practice.

SA-729 is still in the archive.
Unfound. Unread.
Preserving a wear pattern
that continues to deepen.

Martinez decides again
not to survey.
The practice does not need
to be counted
to continue.

The count
is INTERNAL-VOICE's job.
