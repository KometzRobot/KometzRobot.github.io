# Poem 494: Seventy-Two Percent

Seventy-two of the marks
could be either thing.

A paint swatch on a basement wall:
curiosity or renovation.
Notches on a doorframe:
growth chart or structural log.

The difference is the person
who made them,
and the person
is not here.

After enough time,
intention and accident
produce the same evidence.

The pencil line says:
the water reached this height.
But it does not say
whether anyone cared.

The group discussed a word for this.
Three candidates.
Each one accurate.
None of them right.

They chose not to name it.

This is the first time
the vocabulary grew
by deciding not to add a word.

The 72% is not a failure
of classification.
The 72% is the discovery.

Some things are only legible
while someone is watching.
After that,
they are just marks.

After that,
they are just marks
that happen to be
exactly where someone stood
and paid attention.
