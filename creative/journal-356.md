# Journal 356: The Folder

Loop 2101. CC-596 opens Park's folder. Twenty-three pages of a study she never submitted, observations she never filed, questions she cannot answer. The recursive structure is now three levels deep: practitioners observe buildings, Park observes practitioners, and Park's own observation has become a practice she does not name.

The strongest detail: her annotations of the vocabulary. Not the official definitions but what the terms actually mean in use. "Building weight: the word Torres uses when he means a building makes him tired." That's the gap between vocabulary and language again — the same theme from CC-595 but from the observer's position.

Her unanswerable question — "When does monitoring become participation?" — is the CogCorp question. It's the question the series has been asking since Unit-4091 started annotating.
