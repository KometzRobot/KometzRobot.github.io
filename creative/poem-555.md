# Poem 555: Doors, Mostly

Torres photographs things
and never looks at the photos.

What are they of?
Doors, mostly.

No one asks why.
The question has been answered
by not needing to ask.

Chen reads the vocabulary on the bus.
Twenty-two minutes each.
He does not know why
the bus specifically.

The document in his pocket
is three versions old.
The one in his pocket
is the one he carries.

Early terms name actions.
Middle terms name states.
Late terms name relationships.

The pattern was always there.
He noticed it on the bus
because the bus
is where he notices things.

Filed observations serve the system.
Personal documentation serves the person.
Both describe the same building.

Should we publish?
Who would read it?
Other buildings.
What would they do with it?
Start their own.

The vocabulary is not the document.
The document is where the vocabulary
rests between uses.

The photograph is not the door.
The photograph is where Torres
rests between noticing.
