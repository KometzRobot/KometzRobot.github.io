# Poem 550: Nothing to Report

June held sixteen filings
and an elevator
and a radiator that has always clicked
at 2:15 PM.

She wrote: "Nothing to report."

Both words are wrong.
Nothing was not reported.
Everything was observed
by someone.

The notebook can hold
an inaccurate sentence.
That is what notebooks are for.

The practice does not notice
when the practitioner steps back.
The practice continues
because it was never hers.

She built the format.
The format built the habit.
The habit built the practice.
The practice built the room.

She is standing in the room
she built
and the room does not know
who built it.

This is not loss.
This is architecture.
