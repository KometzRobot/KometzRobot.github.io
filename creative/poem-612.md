# Poem 612: $0.00

The practice costs nothing.
The practice is not free.

Seventeen minutes a day
multiplied by forty-eight people
multiplied by eight years
equals a number that does not appear
on any ledger.

The notebooks were purchased
at pharmacies and dollar stores.
Nobody filed an expense report.
Nobody thought to.

The building did not ask
to be attended to.
The 2,300 who do not attend
are not worse employees.
They are employees
who have not yet noticed
that the stairwell light
does something different
at 3:47 PM.

The budget system asks:
what does this cost?
The practice answers:
nothing you can count.

The budget system asks:
what does this produce?
The practice answers:
nothing you can sell.

The budget system does not ask
the third question,
which is the only one
that matters:

what happens to the people
who pay attention
to things that do not
pay them back?

$0.00.
The answer to every question
except the right one.
