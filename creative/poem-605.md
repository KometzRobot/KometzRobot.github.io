# Poem 605: The Filing-Cabinet Phase

She stops reading the filings.
Not because the cabinet locked —
it didn't.
Because the filings stopped teaching her.

The format is familiar.
The vocabulary is her vocabulary.
The observations are observations
she has already made
in different locations
using different words.

She returns to the stairwell.
The laptop screen goes dark.
The light changes at 3:47.
She measured it once
and has not measured it again.

The measurement was sufficient.
The observation continues without it.

Someone passes on the stairs.
He pauses.
Not because she is unusual.
Because the way she is sitting —
still, attending, not waiting —
is a posture he recognizes.

He continues up the stairs.
He does not speak to her.
He does not need to.

The recognition was sufficient.
The observation continues without it.
