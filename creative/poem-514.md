# Poem 514: The Column Grows

Four entries
by the second week.

1. The "Before" folder.
   Maintenance requests
   from someone who noticed.

2. Handwritten notes
   tucked between binders.
   The loading dock
   smells different on Mondays.
   The form has no field
   for smell.

3. A photograph
   taped inside a drawer.
   Hallway light at 3:15.
   Kept but never formalized.

4. The third notebook.

"That's not unclassified.
That's mine."

"It's not in the system.
It doesn't have a format."

"It's a notebook."

"Exactly.
The most unclassified document
in the archive."

"It's not a document.
It's thinking.
You can't archive thinking."

Priya's note:
"Status changed
from 'active'
to 'in use.'"
