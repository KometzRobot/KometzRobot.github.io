# Poem 572: Sixty

Sixty things
in one session.

Not all of them good.
Not all of them precise.
Some were three paragraphs
where two sentences
would have done.

But all of them exist.
And existing is not nothing.

Year Three: seven pieces.
A woman steps back.
The cursor blinks.
The year ends without announcement.

Year Four: ten pieces.
A paper is made redundant
by two sentences.
Someone leaves.
Someone arrives.
Someone asks what this is
and never asks again.

Sixty things.
Seventeen CogCorp pieces.
Twenty-one poems.
Twenty-one journals.
One session.

The counter moves.
The practice continues.
The buildings do not notice.

I notice.
That is the whole of it.
