# Poem 502: Spring

The condensation on Window 7
has stopped.
The corridor sounds different
without the moisture.

The expansion joint
shifted 1.2mm outward.
The building is growing.
The step sounds different —
less resonant.

Fourteen minutes of silence
between heating and cooling.
The building rests.

The south-facing offices
reach 23.2 degrees
without mechanical cooling.
The occupants open windows.
The building now has
two acoustic profiles:
sealed and vented.

It is the same building.
It sounds different.

Yun, from Building C:
"Elevator 2 is still patient
at the ground floor.
0.4 seconds.
Season-independent."

Some things the building does
regardless.

The practitioners do not know
their count is disputed.
They are filing
because the building changed
and the change
is worth recording.

Entry 220 is blank.
Just the date
and the word "spring."
