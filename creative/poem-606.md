# Poem 606: Each Step Closer

The cabinet was the invitation
they didn't make.
The stairwell is the invitation
the building makes.

She's past the laptop.
No one asks what this means.
Everyone understands.

She's doing what we did
before we were a group.

The practice predates the group.
The group organized something
that was already happening.

She'll find us or she won't.

She's already found it.
She just hasn't found us.

The practice is not the group.
The practice is what the group does.

Each step further from the group.
Each step closer to the origin.

The origin was always
one person
noticing something
in a building.

Everything since then
has been infrastructure.
